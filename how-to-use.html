How to Use ElectraAI

Follow these simple steps to get a complete AI-powered diagnostic report for your electronics device in under 2 minutes.

Full Diagnostic — Step by Step
Step 0 — One Time Setup

Enter Your Groq API Key

Click the Settings (⚙️) icon in the top-right header. Enter your free Groq API key (starts with gsk_) and click Save. Your key is stored only in your browser.

Get a free API key at console.groq.com/keys — no credit card needed.
Step 1

Select Your Device

On the main screen, choose the category of your device — Laptop, Mobile, TV, AC, Refrigerator, Washing Machine, and 15+ more options.

Click on the device card that matches your device. It will highlight in cyan when selected.

Can't find your device? Choose the closest category — the AI will still give accurate results.
Step 2

Fill Device Details

Enter your device's Brand (e.g. Samsung, HP, LG) and Model Number. Optionally add the purchase year and any error codes shown on screen.

Select all symptoms that apply to your device from the checklist. Then describe the problem in detail in the text box — the more detail, the better the diagnosis.

Use the microphone button to speak your problem description instead of typing.
Step 3 — Optional but Recommended

Upload Device Photos

Upload photos of your device — the screen, error messages, damaged parts, or internal PCB. This helps the AI give a more accurate visual diagnosis.

You can drag & drop images, browse files, or use your camera directly from the app.

Take clear, well-lit photos. Blurry or dark images reduce AI accuracy.
Step 4

Run AI Diagnostic

Click 'Run AI Diagnostic'. The AI will analyze all your inputs — symptoms, device info, and images — and generate a complete diagnostic report in 10–30 seconds.

You'll see a live scanning animation while the AI processes your data.

Step 5

Review Your Results

Your diagnostic dashboard will show:

  • 🔎 Diagnostic Findings — likely faults with confidence scores
  • 💵 Repair Cost Breakdown — min/max estimates per component
  • 🛠️ Troubleshooting Steps — DIY or technician action guides
  • 🤖 AI Chat — ask follow-up questions about your repair
  • 📍 Nearby Repair Centers — shops near your location
Click "Print / PDF" to save or print your full diagnostic report.

Pro Tips for Better Results

Be Descriptive

The more detail you provide in the problem description, the more accurate the AI diagnosis will be.

Select All Symptoms

Check every symptom that applies — even minor ones. They help the AI narrow down the root cause.

Upload Multiple Photos

Upload photos from different angles — front, back, screen, and internal board if accessible.

Share Location

Allow location access in Step 2 to get real nearby repair shops within 10km of you.

Use History

All past diagnoses are saved. Click the History icon to revisit or compare previous reports.

Chat with AI

After diagnosis, use the AI chat panel to ask specific questions about your repair or parts.
