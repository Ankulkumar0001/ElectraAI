What is ElectraAI?

ElectraAI is an intelligent electronics fault detection system designed for everyday users, repair technicians, and electronics enthusiasts. It uses advanced AI models to analyze device symptoms, uploaded images, and error codes to generate accurate diagnostic reports.

Whether your laptop won't boot, your AC isn't cooling, or your washing machine is making strange noises — ElectraAI walks you through the diagnosis step by step and gives you actionable repair guidance.

Key Features

AI Diagnostics

Powered by Groq's LLaMA 3.3 70B model for deep, accurate fault analysis across 20+ device types.

Image Scan

Upload a photo of your device and AI will visually detect faults from the image using LLaMA 4 Scout.

Cost Estimates

Get realistic repair cost breakdowns with part-level pricing based on Indian market rates.

Nearby Shops

Share your location to find certified repair centers within 10km of your area.

Diagnosis History

All your past diagnoses are saved locally with images so you can revisit them anytime.

PWA Support

Install ElectraAI on your phone or desktop like a native app — works offline too.

ElectraAI is an independent project built with passion for making electronics repair accessible to everyone in India. It is completely free, ad-light, and privacy-respecting. Your data never leaves your device — all history is stored locally in your browser.
